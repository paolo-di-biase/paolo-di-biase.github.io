Paolo Di Biase


Paolo Di Biase is a PhD student at Gran Sasso Science Institute (GSSI), in Computer Science.

Paolo earned his bachelor's degree in 2023 from Unimol. That same year, he began his master's degree in Software Systems Security, where he began working in the field of program analysis and software verification.
In January 2025, he began collaborating with the Program Analysis in the Clouds Lab (PAC Lab), winning a research grant.
Subsequently, to further his studies for his master's thesis, in February 2025 he participated in the Erasmus+ Traineeship project, completing a two-month internship at Diffblue in Oxford, UK.
In October 2025, he earned his master's degree and, finally, in November 2025, he became a PhD student at GSSI.

Research Interests

  • Program analysis and software verification
  • Model checking: algorithms and tools
  • Verification of Concurrent Software
  • SAT/SMT solvers


Current Research Projects

  • CBMC parallelization
  • Sat Solvers optimization (SAT modulo SAT)
  • Model checking of concurrent program using sequentializzation and partial order (Lazy-PO)


Software/Tools

  • Iekke: BMC for concurrent C programs
